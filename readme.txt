ATResearchProject is an ATContentType that has been developed at the Ecology Centre, Kiel University. 
Its purpose is representing information that is related to acquired scientific research projects. 

The coding has been mainly performed by Mike Gabriel <m.gabriel at sunweavers.net>. The Product
is published under the GPL, so feel free to use and modify it according to GPL license terms.

ATResearchProject has been developed under Zope 2.8.1 and Plone 2.1. Downwards compatibility is not intended.

The translations are - for now - available in German only.
